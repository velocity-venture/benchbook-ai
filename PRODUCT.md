# BenchBook AI
## AI-Powered Legal Intelligence for Juvenile Court Judges

---

## Product Vision

BenchBook AI is a specialized legal tech platform designed specifically for Tennessee Juvenile Court Judges. It combines AI-powered legal research, case management, and compliance tracking in a secure, judge-focused interface.

**Unique Advantage:** Built by a sitting Juvenile Court Judge who understands the exact pain points, workflows, and requirements of the role.

---

## Target Market

### Primary
- Tennessee Juvenile Court Judges (~100 across 95 counties)
- Average tech adoption: Moderate (many still use paper/Word)
- Willingness to pay: HIGH for tools that save time and reduce liability

### Secondary
- General Sessions Court Judges (TN)
- Juvenile Court Judges (other states)
- Court Clerks and administrative staff

---

## Core Features (MVP)

### 1. AI Legal Research Assistant
- Natural language queries: "What's the standard for detention in TN?"
- Complete Title 36 & 37 TN Code Annotated (T.C.A.) integration
- Case law search and citation
- Juvenile-specific legal research

### 2. Case Docket Dashboard
- Daily/weekly case calendar
- Quick case lookup by name or number
- Status tracking (pending, continued, disposed)
- Upcoming deadlines and reviews

### 3. Smart Case Notes
- Structured templates for juvenile cases
- AI-assisted note generation
- Confidentiality flags (FERPA compliance)
- Search across all cases

### 4. Document Generation
- Detention orders
- Disposition orders
- Transfer orders
- Custom templates

### 5. Compliance Tracking
- FERPA compliance reminders
- Detention timeline tracking
- Review date calculations
- Audit trail

---

## Technical Stack

- **Frontend:** Next.js 14, Tailwind CSS, shadcn/ui
- **Backend:** Supabase (PostgreSQL, Auth, Storage)
- **AI:** OpenAI GPT-4o for legal research
- **Hosting:** Vercel or Cloudflare Pages
- **Security:** SOC 2 compliance path, encryption at rest
