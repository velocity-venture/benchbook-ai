# TCA Title 39: Criminal Offenses

**STATUS: STUB - Content needed before this title is indexed**

## How to Obtain

Download from Tennessee General Assembly (https://www.tn.gov/content/tn/code.html) or
from LexisNexis/Westlaw if the court has a subscription.

Save as plain text or HTML and the prebuild script will pick it up automatically.

## Priority Sections for General Sessions

- **39-11**: General Provisions (criminal responsibility, defenses, mental state)
- **39-13**: Offenses Against the Person (assault, domestic assault, aggravated assault, homicide)
- **39-14**: Offenses Against Property (theft, vandalism, burglary, arson)
- **39-15**: Offenses Against the Family (child abuse, contributing to delinquency, abandonment)
- **39-17**: Offenses Against Public Health, Safety and Welfare (drugs, weapons, DUI, public intoxication)
