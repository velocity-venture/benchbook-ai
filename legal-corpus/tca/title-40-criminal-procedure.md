# TCA Title 40: Criminal Procedure

**STATUS: STUB - Content needed before this title is indexed**

## How to Obtain

Download from Tennessee General Assembly (https://www.tn.gov/content/tn/code.html) or
from LexisNexis/Westlaw if the court has a subscription.

## Priority Sections for General Sessions

- **40-1**: Preliminary Proceedings (jurisdiction, venue)
- **40-7**: Arrest (powers of arrest, warrants, citation release)
- **40-11**: Bail/Bond (bond schedules, conditions of release, bond revocation, appearance bonds)
- **40-14**: Preliminary Examination (probable cause hearings)
- **40-15**: Grand Jury (indictment, presentment)
- **40-32**: Expungement (eligibility, procedures, judicial review)
- **40-33**: Probation (conditions, revocation procedures, hearings)
- **40-35**: Criminal Sentencing Reform Act (sentencing ranges, alternatives, enhancement factors)
- **40-36**: Community Corrections (alternatives to incarceration)
